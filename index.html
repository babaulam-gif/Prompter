The Framework
6 Components of a Professional Prompt
01 — ROLE
Role / Persona
Who should the AI act as? A persona shapes expertise, tone, and depth.
"Act as a senior financial advisor..."
02 — TASK
Task / Instruction
The specific action. What exactly should the AI do with your input?
"Summarise", "Translate", "Draft", "Analyse"
03 — CONTEXT
Context
Background the AI needs — audience, purpose, situation, or constraints.
"The audience is beginners with no prior knowledge..."
04 — INPUT
Input Data
The actual content to process — or a placeholder for reusable templates.
"Text: [Paste your article here]"
05 — CONSTRAINT
Constraint
Boundaries and requirements — tone, length, language, style, or limits.
"Keep under 150 words. Avoid jargon. Be direct."
06 — FORMAT
Output Format
How the response should be structured, styled, or presented.
"Bullet list", "3 paragraphs", "Table", "JSON"
